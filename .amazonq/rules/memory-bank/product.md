# Farm Produce Marketplace

## Project Purpose
A comprehensive digital marketplace connecting farmers directly with buyers, eliminating intermediaries and enabling fair trade of fresh agricultural products.

## Value Proposition
- **Direct Trade**: Farmers sell directly to buyers, maximizing profits and ensuring freshness
- **Real-time Communication**: Built-in chat system for instant buyer-farmer communication
- **Quality Assurance**: Rating and review system to maintain product quality standards
- **Inventory Management**: Real-time stock tracking and automated order processing
- **Secure Transactions**: JWT-based authentication and secure payment integration

## Key Features
- **Multi-role System**: Separate dashboards for farmers and buyers with role-specific functionality
- **Product Management**: Full CRUD operations for product listings with categories and search
- **Order Processing**: Complete order lifecycle from creation to delivery tracking
- **Payment Integration**: M-Pesa payment gateway for seamless transactions
- **Rating System**: Comprehensive review and rating system for quality control
- **Real-time Chat**: Direct messaging between buyers and farmers
- **Leaderboard**: Gamification features to encourage quality and engagement
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first approach with modern UI/UX

## Target Users
- **Farmers**: Small to medium-scale agricultural producers seeking direct market access
- **Buyers**: Consumers, restaurants, and retailers looking for fresh, quality produce
- **Agricultural Cooperatives**: Groups of farmers wanting to expand their market reach

## Use Cases
- Farmers listing seasonal produce with real-time inventory updates
- Buyers browsing and purchasing fresh produce with delivery coordination
- Quality feedback loop through ratings improving overall marketplace standards
- Direct communication for custom orders and bulk purchases
- Market price transparency and fair trade practices
